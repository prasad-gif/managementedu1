CMAT EXAM PATTERN

NTA has changed their pattern for CMAT by adding an optional section for students who have passion about innovation and entrepreneurship and aspire to study further. This section is called entrepreneurship and innovation. candidates must go through the CMAT paper pattern thoroughly and setup their strategies likewise.

CMAT exam pattern and structure.
Conducting body NTA
Exam mode Computer based
Total number of questions 100
Total number of questions per section

VARC-25

DILR-25

QA-25

GK-25
Total number of questions with additional section 125
Total marks 400
Total marks with additional section 500
Sectional time limit No

Total duration 3 hrs(180 min)
Additional section Optional
