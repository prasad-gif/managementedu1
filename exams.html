ALL ABOUT CAT ENTRANCE

MBA CAT is one of the highly recommended MBA entrance exam for students who aspire to purse MBA in top B-schools. It is one of the highly rigorous entrance examinations for MBA in INDIA. It is a way to many top colleges in INDIA. IIM’S – INDIAN INSTITUTE OF MANAGEMENT STUDIES are considered the best colleges for MBA and all these IIM’S accept CAT score. Apart from IIM’S there are many several other colleges that are ranked higher than some IIM’S. these colleges include FMS DELHI, ISB HYDERABAD, MDI GURGAON, IIT DELHI, IIFT, SPJIMR and many more.

ALL ABOUT CMAT ENTRANCE

Common Management Admission Test (CMAT) is an online computer-based test conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA), India. It is a national-level admission test for facilitating institutions to select suitable students for admission in all management programmes approved by AICTE. CMAT is also a way to many top colleges in INDIA.

ALL ABOUT ATMA ENTRANCE

AIMS Test for Management Admissions or ATMA is a national-level exam conducted by the Association of Indian Management Schools (AIMS) for admission to MBA, PGDM, MCA and PGDBA courses. ... More than 500 management institutes across India accept ATMA scores for admission to their management programmes.

ALL ABOUT SNAP ENTRANCE

SNAP is a top level management examination. SNAP is conducted by symbosis and is a national level aptitude test. A candidate aspiring to join an MBA programme offered by any Institute of Symbiosis International (Deemed University) [SIU] has to take the common, mandatory Symbiosis National Aptitude (SNAP) Computer Based Test (CBT) 2020.
